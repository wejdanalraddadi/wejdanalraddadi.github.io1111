Improving Attendance and Understanding the Customers For Boston Red Sox

Boston Red Sox has huge datasets in different formats regarding customers, Game and Ticket Data. The department needed those data extracted, cleaned & joined to Analysis data and create machine learning models to answer Three questions: What types of tickets sold poorly year over year (YoY)? What impact does ticket price change have on attendance? What causes a game to have poor attendance?
Findings and Conclusions:
  • Lower-priced seats are the worst-selling, and have also decreased between 2018 to 2019 on a per game basis.
  • A negative correlation of average attendance YoY with price change YoY by section. This price sensitivity appears strongest in the lower-priced seats .
  • The biggest external factor to a game having above average attendance is the weather. 54.5 degrees Fahrenheit is a key cutoff point - any temperature below negatively affects attendance .

Tools Used:
  • SAS.
  • SQL.
  • Advanced Excel (PivotTables And VLOOKUP).
  • Power BI.
